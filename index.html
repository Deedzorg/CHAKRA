About CHAKRA

CHAKRA: Game Overview

Welcome to CHAKRA – a strategic board game of wits, tactics, and clever moves! In CHAKRA you compete against human or computer opponents on a uniquely designed board. Whether you’re aiming to fill your target zone or eliminate your foes, every move counts.

Objective:

  • Primary Goal: Maneuver your pieces across the board to occupy all the nodes in your designated target area.
  • Alternate Win: Eliminate all opponent pieces via jump moves.
